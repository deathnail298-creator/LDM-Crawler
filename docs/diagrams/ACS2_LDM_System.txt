=======================================

This ACS2 map describes the static subsystem architecture, internal routing,
and environmental interaction pathways for the Lunar Dust Mitigator (LDM).

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
NODES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

CHASSIS_CORE
    - Monolithic structural frame
    - Load-bearing geometry
    - Houses all internal subsystems

ELECTRONICS_COFFIN
    - Sealed, potted electronics block
    - Contains MCU/timer, HV driver, power management

HV_PLATE_ARRAY
    - Fixed-bias electrostatic lift plates
    - Embedded beneath dielectric top surface

DIELECTRIC_TOP_LAYER
    - Protective coating over HV_PLATE_ARRAY
    - Interfaces with regolith and lofted dust

PASSIVE_AGITATION_GEOMETRY
    - Terraces, micro-channels, basins
    - Primary dust-collision and conditioning zone

SOLAR_PANEL
    - Fixed-orientation photovoltaic surface
    - Primary power source

BATTERY_BANK
    - Small, slow-cycling Li-ion or LFP pack
    - Deep-sleep majority operation

POWER_BUS
    - Connects SOLAR_PANEL and BATTERY_BANK to ELECTRONICS_COFFIN

THERMAL_PATHS
    - Conductive links through chassis
    - Ties ELECTRONICS_COFFIN to radiative surfaces

RADIATOR_SURFACES
    - Passive radiators integrated into outer geometry

ENVIRONMENT_INTERFACE
    - Direct contact surface with lunar regolith and vacuum
    - Includes dust loft/fall region and regolith drift zone


---------------------------------------------------------------------------
EDGES (Functional & Physical Links)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

CHASSIS_CORE  -> ELECTRONICS_COFFIN
    Structural containment + mechanical isolation

CHASSIS_CORE  -> THERMAL_PATHS
    Conduction channels molded/machined into frame

THERMAL_PATHS -> RADIATOR_SURFACES
    Steady-state and cyclic heat rejection route

SOLAR_PANEL   -> POWER_BUS
    Primary daytime charging

BATTERY_BANK  -> POWER_BUS
    Nighttime and operational-cycle support

POWER_BUS     -> ELECTRONICS_COFFIN
    Provides power to MCU/timer and HV driver

ELECTRONICS_COFFIN -> HV_PLATE_ARRAY
    High-voltage activation during charge cycles

HV_PLATE_ARRAY -> DIELECTRIC_TOP_LAYER
    Electric field transmitted through dielectric to loft dust

DIELECTRIC_TOP_LAYER -> PASSIVE_AGITATION_GEOMETRY
    Lofted dust falls onto terraces and channels

PASSIVE_AGITATION_GEOMETRY -> ENVIRONMENT_INTERFACE
    Conditioned dust migrates outward into local regolith

ENVIRONMENT_INTERFACE -> PASSIVE_AGITATION_GEOMETRY
    Fresh unconditioned dust drifts inward naturally

ENVIRONMENT_INTERFACE -> DIELECTRIC_TOP_LAYER
    Dust coverage modifies field efficiency over mission


---------------------------------------------------------------------------
MODE BEHAVIORAL RELATIONSHIPS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

MODE_SLEEP
    - ELECTRONICS_COFFIN idle
    - HV_PLATE_ARRAY inactive
    - SOLAR_PANEL charges BATTERY_BANK
    - No agitation beyond natural regolith movement

MODE_LIFT
    - ELECTRONICS_COFFIN activates HV_PLATE_ARRAY
    - DIELECTRIC_TOP_LAYER charges surface particles
    - ENVIRONMENT_INTERFACE lofts dust upward

MODE_COLLISION
    - HV off
    - Dust descends
    - PASSIVE_AGITATION_GEOMETRY forces collisions and rolling

MODE_REDISTRIBUTION
    - Conditioned dust drifts outward
    - Fresh dust drifts inward


---------------------------------------------------------------------------
FAILURE STATE MAP (Structural)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Loss of HV_PLATE_ARRAY
    -> System defaults to MODE_SLEEP indefinitely

Loss of SOLAR_PANEL
    -> BATTERY_BANK drains -> permanent MODE_SLEEP

Loss of PASSIVE_AGITATION_GEOMETRY (erosion/fracture)
    -> Reduced conditioning efficiency; still safe

Burial by Regolith
    -> All nodes isolated; system becomes inert terrain object
